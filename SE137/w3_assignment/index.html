My Bio

Born and raised in Rhode Island, I grew up in Pawtucket and Lincoln where my only concerns were sports, video games and music.Frequent childhood trips to Queens, NY to visit family would eventually influence the decision to move there myself. I started recording and making music and decided to attend audio school in Manhattan. I'd end up teaching myself photography, videography and graphic design and while I don't consider myself a professional in any of those, I'd say I'm moderately proficient in each. Some time later, I found work at the Javits Center as part of Teamsters and spent several years there. The pandemic basically shut down the trade show industry and left me without work indefinitely. This forced me to begin to look at other career options and ultimately deciding on returning to school to learn a new trade where I initially joined for cybersecurity but soon switched over to software engineering.
